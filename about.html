About Mindtest

We believe mental health awareness starts with self-knowledge. Our mission is to make evidence-based mental health screening tools free, accessible, and private for everyone — no matter where they are.

🎯 Our Mission

Mental health is just as important as physical health, yet millions of people lack access to basic screening tools. Mindtest was created to bridge this gap by providing:

  • Evidence-based mental health assessments grounded in established psychological research
  • Complete anonymity — no real names, emails, or personal data required
  • 100% free access to professional-grade screening tools
  • Privacy-first design with local data storage
  • Accessible in multiple languages to serve a global community

📊 What We Offer

Mindtest provides five comprehensive mental health screening assessments:

  • Mental Health Assessment — Evaluates mood, anxiety, sleep quality, energy levels, and cognitive functioning over the past two weeks
  • Personality Test — Explores your personality traits across five key dimensions: extraversion, conscientiousness, neuroticism, openness, and agreeableness
  • Stress Level Test — Measures your perceived stress levels, physical stress responses, coping abilities, and emotional resilience
  • Social Anxiety Screen — Screens for social anxiety tendencies including performance anxiety, physical responses, and social avoidance behaviors
  • Sleep Quality Assessment — Evaluates your sleep patterns including falling asleep, sleep maintenance, daytime function, and sleep routine

💎 Our Values

Privacy is fundamental. Unlike most health platforms, we do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any personally identifiable information. Your assessment answers are stored locally on your device and never uploaded to any server.

Mental health awareness should be universal. Every tool we provide is completely free. We believe that access to mental health screening is a right, not a privilege.

Science-based, not guesswork. Our assessments are built using validated screening instruments used in clinical research, including modified PHQ-9, GAD-7, PSS, and other evidence-based tools.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is Mindtest a replacement for professional diagnosis? No. Mindtest assessments are for self-awareness and educational purposes only. They do not constitute medical diagnosis or treatment advice. If you are experiencing significant psychological distress, please consult a licensed professional.
  • Is my data safe? Yes. All assessment answers are stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. No cookies are used for tracking.
  • Is it really free? Yes, Mindtest is and always will be 100% free. We are supported by advertising revenue to keep the platform accessible.
  • Can I trust the results? Our assessments are based on validated psychological screening instruments. However, they are not diagnostic tools. For clinical diagnosis, please consult a qualified mental health professional.
