Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Information Collection

We collect only the following information when you register: username and password (encrypted). We do not collect your real name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information. When you use the assessment features, your answers are stored locally in your browser (localStorage) and are not uploaded to any server.

2. Use of Information

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing account login and assessment history features
  • Improving and optimizing the user experience
  • Generating anonymous usage statistics (visible in the admin panel)

3. Cookies

We use localStorage to save your login status and interface preferences (such as theme). This platform does not use tracking cookies.

4. Advertising

This platform displays third-party advertisements. Some advertisers may use cookies and similar technologies to serve personalized ads. We do not share your personal information with advertisers.

5. Disclaimer

The mental health assessment results provided on this platform are for reference only and do not constitute any medical diagnosis or treatment advice. If you are experiencing significant psychological distress, please seek help from a professional counselor or doctor.

7. Policy Updates
